# Icanpreneur Discovery and Validation Lane

## Purpose

`Icanpreneur` is Chummer's bounded adaptive discovery-interview and validation lane.
It exists to collect high-signal user demand without letting interview tooling become design canon, feature truth, or backlog ownership.

The first flagship use is KARMA FORGE house-rule discovery, but the lane also applies to:

* GM Companion / Switch persona validation
* BLACK LEDGER world-state validation
* TABLE PULSE trust and coaching validation
* creator publication and artifact-workflow validation
* launch and onboarding message validation

## Owner split

* canonical lane policy: `chummer6-design`
* interview synthesis and packet normalization: `executive-assistant`
* intake and follow-up routing: `chummer6-hub`
* decision ownership: Product Governor

## Allowed uses

* AI-led or AI-assisted interviews
* role-specific interview tracks
* synthesis of repeated pains and workarounds
* persona updates
* message and concept validation
* discovery closeouts that feed Chummer-owned packets

## Forbidden uses

* direct rule-package generation
* direct implementation-priority decisions
* direct backlog ownership
* direct canon mutation
* copyrighted-book-text capture
* becoming the system of record for feature requests

## Canonical output types

The lane may produce only Chummer-owned normalized outputs such as:

* `HouseRuleDemandPacket`
* `KarmaForgeCandidate`
* `RuleEnvironmentImpactHypothesis`
* `CompanionPersonaFinding`
* `WorldGameDemandPacket`
* `TriggerTolerabilityFinding`

Raw interview transcripts, vendor-side scoring, or vendor-side summaries are never canonical by themselves.

## Required conversion rule

Every meaningful Icanpreneur result that enters product work must become one of:

* a Chummer-owned packet
* a Chummer-owned decision record
* a Chummer-owned backlog candidate
* a Chummer-owned rejection or defer decision with reasons

## Consent and IP boundary

Every participant must know:

* what is being collected
* why it is being collected
* whether it may inform Chummer design
* whether quotes may be used
* how removal or anonymization works

Users should not be asked to paste raw copyrighted rulebook passages.

## KARMA FORGE first move

For KARMA FORGE, Icanpreneur should uncover the actual demand behind the user's wording.

Example:

* user wording: "let me override gear availability"
* likely need: "campaign-scoped availability overlays with build-impact preview, player-visible receipts, and later unlocks from run outcomes"

## Track families

Current intended track families:

* GM house-rule discovery
* player trust discovery
* creator/publisher rule-pack discovery
* organizer / BLACK LEDGER discovery
* Chummer5a veteran / migration discovery
