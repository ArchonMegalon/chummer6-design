# Product Vision Recovery and Integration Guide

**Product:** Chummer6 / SR Campaign OS  
**Status:** Active successor-wave overlay  
**Purpose:** Turn the product-vision audit into explicit design canon and queued product work so the program closes lost potential, not just missing proof.

## Product rule

Chummer6 should not chase feature-count sprawl.
It should become the calm, trustworthy, continuity-rich campaign OS that serious Shadowrun tables feel missing when they go back to anything less integrated.

That means five things must become product truth rather than design aspiration:

1. calm-under-pressure help during play
2. veteran-grade dense workflow confidence
3. boring desktop trust and recovery proof
4. mobile and companion continuity between sessions
5. artifact, community, and publication loops that feel like extensions of campaign truth rather than adjacent sidecars

## What users still want or miss

The audit says users still want:

* "Tell me what I can do right now, why, and what rule or source backs it."
* "Adopt my current campaign without rebuilding my table from scratch."
* "Keep campaign memory, consequences, and runner goals alive between sessions."
* "Make dense veteran workflows feel as fast and familiar as Chummer5A."
* "Give me trusted mobile or companion continuity for travel, recap, and return moments."
* "Let me publish, recap, share, and form tables from the same canonical campaign truth."

Those are not abstract UX wishes.
They are the emotional center of gravity for why Chummer should feel indispensable.

## Repo-grounded recovery lanes

### 1. Calm-under-pressure loop

The campaign OS promise is still under-materialized until one player and one GM can move through:

```text
open the current state
-> see action budget and explain anchor
-> finish the turn
-> close the run
-> update one runner goal
-> receive one approved consequence
```

This lane is about immediate clarity, not world-simulation breadth.

### 2. Veteran-depth parity

The parity bar is still too broad and not deep enough where veterans actually feel friction.

The remaining release-shaping families are:

* translator and XML amendment routes
* dense builder and career workflows
* dice, initiative, and table utilities
* import oracles and Hero Lab paths
* contacts, lifestyles, history, and related continuity dialogs
* print, export, and multi-runner utility flows

These families need direct screenshot-backed and runtime-backed proof.
Aggregate readiness must not stand in for family-level parity.

### 3. Trust shelf and executable proof

The trust story is only honest when the shipped bytes, startup-smoke receipts, release-channel truth, and recovery posture all agree.

If a promoted installer cannot be proven from the same promoted bytes, the trust promise is still open.

### 4. Mobile and companion continuity

Mobile is not a second-class afterthought.
It is the continuity lane for:

* recap
* return moments
* player-safe consequence feed
* travel posture
* degraded-network continuity

Until those moments feel dependable, Chummer remains desk-strong but habit-weak.

### 5. Artifact and community coherence

JACKPOINT, RUNBOOK PRESS, GHOSTWIRE, RUNSITE, TABLE PULSE, and Community Hub should not read as disconnected horizons.
They should feel like coherent extensions of:

* build truth
* campaign truth
* recap truth
* publication truth
* community formation truth

The product should make one table feel:

* remembered
* explainable
* publishable
* reconnectable

### 6. Gate discipline for human-feeling parity

Readiness cannot rely only on broad journey pass/fail.
The gate stack must prove the exact dialogs and sub-workflows that experienced users actually judge:

* dense-builder rhythm
* import and migration rails
* roster and continuity dialogs
* utility and initiative surfaces
* publication and export affordances

If a surface is not directly proven, it should stay open.

## Integration posture for owned or future-acquired LTDs

These lanes are product-fit opportunities, not excuses to let external systems become hidden truth owners.

### 1. Community scheduling and roster formation

Opportunity:

* Open Runs
* Community Hub
* campaign-group movement
* organizer operations

Potential fit:

* scheduling and roster-formation LTDs
* table-formation or lightweight event-intake assets

Boundary:

* external tools may enrich scheduling and formation
* campaign truth, permissions, and audit receipts stay inside Chummer-owned domains

### 2. Creator publication and editorial acceleration

Opportunity:

* RUNBOOK PRESS
* JACKPOINT
* artifact shelf
* creator discovery and trust ranking

Potential fit:

* creator-publication
* editorial workflow
* print or artifact-production assets

Boundary:

* external tooling may accelerate packaging and editorial polish
* publication lineage, revocation, compatibility, and consent stay governed by Chummer truth

### 3. Travel, route, and venue intelligence

Opportunity:

* RUNSITE
* travel continuity
* route packs
* observer moments

Potential fit:

* travel-intelligence
* route-packing
* venue-awareness assets

Boundary:

* external route intelligence may enrich context
* campaign, run, and player-safe continuity remain Chummer truth

### 4. Privacy-safe post-session coaching

Opportunity:

* TABLE PULSE
* recap
* coaching-safe followthrough

Potential fit:

* bounded coaching
* after-action analytics
* privacy-first recap assistants

Boundary:

* coaching may summarize patterns
* it must never override rules, campaign truth, support truth, or public reputation posture

### 5. Manual intake and migration confidence

Opportunity:

* manual intake
* veteran migration
* oracle import
* Hero Lab or adjacent compatibility

Potential fit:

* import normalization
* structured scanning
* migration-confidence tools

Boundary:

* external tooling may accelerate intake and mapping
* canonical runner, campaign, rules, and provenance truth stay inside Chummer

## Program rule

External LTDs or future acquisitions must slot into clean seams.
They may accelerate:

* intake
* scheduling
* publication
* route intelligence
* coaching

They must not become silent owners of:

* rules truth
* campaign truth
* support truth
* release truth
* public trust posture
