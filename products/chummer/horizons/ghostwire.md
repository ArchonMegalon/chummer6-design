# GHOSTWIRE

## Table pain

When something confusing or contested happens in play, the table often cannot reconstruct what actually happened without slowing the session to a crawl or trusting memory.

## Bounded product move

GHOSTWIRE is the replay and forensics horizon.
It would turn append-only session receipts into replay timelines, after-action review, grounded recap artifacts, and bounded what-if comparisons without creating a second session truth.

## Likely owners

* `chummer6-core`
* `chummer6-mobile`
* `chummer6-hub`
* `chummer6-media-factory`

## Key tool posture

* `PeekShot` - replay preview and share-card support
* `Soundmadeseen` - narrated after-action or recap media
* `MarkupGo` - structured after-action document rendering
* `Mootion` - bounded animated recap support
* `Paperguide` - cited research/summarization helper where useful

## Foundations

* canonical session event ledger
* replay-safe reducer semantics
* explain provenance canon
* render-side artifact and preview receipts

## Why still a horizon

Replay is only worth shipping if it can prove what happened without mutating or reinterpreting the canonical ledger.
Until Chummer can guarantee reducer-safe reconstruction, this lane should stay bounded as future-capability canon.
